Foot train

  • Originality: Unique soundtrack composed from my heart. It's two player capability makes it fun for the whole family.
  • Technical Merit: Well balanced RNG trains
  • Prototype Postmortem: I learned a lot about keyboard input, and how testing a multiplayer game is hard.
  • Prototype Assets: Songs made by me. Photos from google searching "bad train picture," "train crossing," and "shoe."
  • Prototype Closest Other Game:
    • The code for this example is adapted from the How to Make Flappy Bird in HTML5 With Phaser - Part 1
  • High Concept: Multiplayer avoid trains as a shoe game.
  • Prototype Goal: Multiplayerability in side scroller avoidance games.
  • Player Experience Goals: Fun and competitiveness to achieve a high score.
  • Gameplay: The player can jump by pressing W or P.
  • Strategies: Staying close to the center to either go up or down.
  • Target Audience: This game is for everyone.
  • Play Time: Maybe a minute or two.
